Slack is one of the available notification channels

1. If you do not have a Slack workspace, go to [Slack](https://slack.com/) and create one.
2. Go to [Slack Apps](https://api.slack.com/apps) and create a new app.
3. Copy the `Client ID`, `Client Secret` and `Signing Secret` and set the following environment variables:

```bash
SLACK_CLIENT_ID=<client-id>
SLACK_CLIENT_SECRET=<client-secret>
SLACK_SIGNING_SECRET=<signing-secret>
```

4. Under **Features** > **Incoming Webhooks**, enable `Activate Incoming Webhooks`.
5. Under **Features** > **OAuth & Permissions** > **Redirect URLs**, add `https://localhost:3000/integrations/slack/callback`
6. Under **Features** > **OAuth & Permissions** > **Scopes** > **Bot token scopes**, add the `incoming-webhook` scope.
